Rice

This is the easiest way to make some tasty rice.

    Ingredients
  • 1 cup of flippin' Rice
  • 2 cups wet Water
    Directions
  1. Gingerly place cup of rice into rice maker.
  2. Toss the 2 cups of water into rice maker.
  3. Turn on rice maker and in 15-20 torturous mins, you're gonna have some awesome rice.
