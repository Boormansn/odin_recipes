Bibimbop

This is a little more involved, but a hearty dish well worth the effort

    Ingredients
  • Half sliced carrot
  • half sliced cucumber
  • 1/3 lb marinated beef
  • 1 cup white rice (already made)
  • 1/4 lb kimchi
    Directions
  1. Place cooked rice, carrot, and cucumber into stone pot on stove. Medium heat
  2. Cook beef seperate on pan. Then add to stone pot
  3. Add kimchi
  4. Eat and enjoy that bad jackson
